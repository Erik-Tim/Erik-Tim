Country Fried Pork Chops

Country Fried Pork Chops are a classic Southern comfort food featuring tender pork chops that are seasoned, breaded, and pan-fried until golden brown and crispy.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb pork chops (bone-in or boneless)
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder
  • 1 cup of oil
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tsp each of salt, pepper, paprika (for flour mix)
  • 1 cup buttermilk

Directions

  1. Pat pork chops dry and season both sides.
  2. Marinate pork chops in buttermilk for 1 or 2 hours.
  3. Mix flour and spices in a bowl for breading.
  4. Heat oil to about 330 degrees in a safe pan.
  5. Remove pork chops from buttermilk and coat in flour mixture.
  6. Carefully place pork chops into hot oil.
  7. Cook until golden brown and fully cooked.
  8. Drain, season lightly, and serve hot.
